JetPeel Scalp&Hair

Lush, healthy hair is a well-known sign of youth. But as we age, we may experience hair loss or thinning, which can lower self-esteem and confidence. Luckily, there is a non-invasive solution that can regenerate hair growth – the innovative JetPeel technology offered by SENSO Skin Experts.

 

Maintaining a nourished, well-kept scalp helps target hair problems at the root. Using needle-free technology, SENSO JetPeel hair regeneration encourages growth while improving quality, appearance, and thickness.


SENSO JetPeel for hair regeneration offers the gift of scalp-skin harmony and is suitable for all hair types. While it offers a world of benefits, this chemical-free treatment is most famous for its effects on hair loss.
